Ghana Education Digital Platform (GEDP)

Ghana Education Digital Platform (GEDP) is an ERP software tailored for Ghana Education Service (GES) with its core mandate, is to PROPERLY STREAMLINE DATA for Ministry of Education for informed decision making.

  • VISION: To properly streamline data for Ministry of Education and Ghana Education Service (GES) to easily foster data accessibility.
  • MISSION: "To evolve from manual process to paperless system"

About GEDP

GEDP is a comprehensive one-in-all ERP software designed to streamline data through administrative and management functions for Ghana Education Service (GES).

Our solution integrates tools for LMS, staff management, payroll processing, admissions, and recruitment into one unified platform.
