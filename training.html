Training & Support

Expert guidance and dedicated support services to ensure you get the most out of the Ghana Education Digital Platform.

GEDP Expert Support

As a GEDP user, you have hands-on technical support and expert guidance to help you master the system in no time – so you can enjoy immediate access to all the benefits the platform has to offer.

App Support

You have access to a dedicated team of experts to provide your management staff, teachers, system admins, or parents with all the support they need on how to access the GEDP system.

Communication Support

In case your school system administrator needs communication support for departments, our team will train them on how to relay information effectively to team members.

Customer Success Consultants

Your Agency appoints its own dedicated GEDP consultant to ensure satisfaction with products and services, including specialized support for different institutional needs.

Implementation Support

We help you onboard all your users and data effortlessly. Our team will assist in setting up your modules of choice to ensure a smooth transition to digital processes.

Need Immediate Assistance?

Our support team is ready to help you with any technical or administrative queries.
