About GEDP

GEDP is a comprehensive one-in-all ERP system, strategically engineered to streamline and digitize core administrative and service-delivery processes across the Ghana Education Service (GES) and to enhance data integrity, and provide actionable insights across various divisions at Ghana Education Service, withinclusion of self applications modules.

The solution integrates tools for LMS, staff management, payroll processing, admissions and recruitment portal and performance tracking all within one unified platform. By automating routine tasks and providing real-time data insights, GEDP enhances operational efficiency, promotes transparency, and supports data-driven decision-making.

GEDP offers smart, industry-tailored solutions to prevent problems and solve existing problems. As the educational landscape evolves, educational agencies and schools require robust, scalable, and intuitive solutions to modernize administrative processes, academic tracking, and overall school management.

GEDP addresses these needs by integrating essential functions into a single platform that supports ministries, GES, school administrators, facilitators, students and pupil to enhance institutional efficiency, promote transparency, and improve communication among all stakeholders ultimately contributing to a higher quality of education.
