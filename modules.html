Our System Modules

Discover the comprehensive suite of tools designed to transform educational administration.

Administrative Modules

Centralized day-to-day operations through a user-friendly dashboard with role-based access. It enables efficient management of records, scheduling, and administrative communications for Ghana Education Service (GES).

Academic Modules

Provides tools for curriculum management, academic tracking, assessments, and performance reporting. This module includes the Transcript Checker, which allows parents and guardians to monitor academic progress each term and for government and private institutions to track academic records of pupil and students.

Staff Management

Facilitates staff related records, workstations and performance reviews. This module streamlines human resource management and supports effective staff administration.

Data Analytics

Data analytics module is designed to help present life-informed data to help in decision making. This module helps users to analyze, and distribute actionable information driven by data analysis. This typically includes information about pupil and students transcripts, staff informations, administrative records, and this information is presented in illustration format to easily made a quality decisions. Having this module helps data analysts format information to develop more effective strategies for all respective agencies.

Learning Management System (LMS)

With the advent of GEDP ERP system, GES will be modernizing their library management systems to meet the evolving needs of pupil, students and facilitators. GEDP ERP enables digital cataloging, efficient resource tracking, and streamlined circulation processes, thus enhancing accessibility to educational materials and fostering a culture of continuous learning. Moreover, learners and facilitators will integrate library systems with other administrative functions.

The LMS feature has interactive tools such as discussion forums, quizzes, and multimedia resources to support diverse teaching and learning styles. By promoting active engagement and collaboration, the LMS enhances the quality of instruction, facilitates personalized learning experiences, and empowers pupils and students to take ownership of their education.
