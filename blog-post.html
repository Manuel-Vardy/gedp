Masterclass: Maximizing Data Analytics for GES

February 9, 2026 By GEDP Editorial Team

Our upcoming training session aims to equip administrators with the skills to interpret complex educational data and transform it into actionable insights for the Ghana Education Service (GES).

In today's rapidly evolving educational landscape, data is more than just numbers; it's a compass for successful policy-making and resource allocation. The Masterclass: Maximizing Data Analytics for GES is designed to bridge the gap between data collection and data utilization.

Key Learning Objectives

Participants will engage with a variety of modules throughout the masterclass, focusing on:

  • Understanding the Data Analytics architecture within the GEDP platform.
  • Techniques for cleaning and preparing institutional data for analysis.
  • Creating visual dashboards that communicate performance trends effectively.
  • Standardizing reporting across different regional blocks.
"The goal is simple: to ensure every decision made at the administrative level is backed by high-integrity, real-time data." — Director of Systems Integration, GEDP.

This masterclass is part of our ongoing commitment to ensure that the Ghana Education Service doesn't just digitize its records, but also adopts a data-driven culture. We believe that by empowering our staff with these advanced analytical tools, we can significantly improve educational outcomes for pupils and students nationwide.
