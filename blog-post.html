Masterclass: Maximizing Data Analytics for GES

News & Update / Article
February 9, 2026 By GEDP Editorial Team

The digital transformation of Ghana's education sector continues to reach new heights as the latest phase of the Ghana Education Digital Platform (GEDP) rollout begins.

Our commitment to excellence in education is reflected in every pixel of our platform. By empowering teachers with advanced data tools and students with accessible digital resources, we are not just changing how people learn; we are changing how they think about their future in a global economy.

The transition to a paperless environment has already shown significant improvements in administrative efficiency across 500 pilot schools. "The difference is night and day," says one local headteacher. "We can now focus more on our students' progress and less on paperwork."

Looking ahead, the GEDP team is preparing for a series of masterclasses designed to equip every level of education management with data literacy. These sessions will ensure that decision-making is driven by real-time insights rather than intuition alone.

As we celebrate these milestones, we remain focused on the millions of learners who are now connected to the world of knowledge through their screens. The journey has just begun, and the potential is limitless.
