GEDP Teacher Upgrading System

The Teacher Upgrading System in Ghana Education Service (GES) is the official process used to move a teacher from a lower professional rank or qualification level to a higher one after obtaining additional academic or professional qualifications.

It is the system that recognizes a teacher's new qualification and upgrades their rank, salary scale, and professional status within GES.

How It Works Apply Now

What is the Teacher Upgrading System?

The Teacher Upgrading System refers to the administrative and digital process used by GES to update a teacher's professional grade after they further their education.

The Teacher Upgrading System in GES is the official process that allows teachers who obtain higher academic qualifications to be promoted to higher professional ranks and salary levels within the Ghana Education Service.

Purpose of the Teacher Upgrading System

The upgrading system was introduced to achieve several goals:

Professional Development

It encourages teachers to continue their education and improve their teaching skills.

Recognition of Higher Qualification

Teachers who obtain new qualifications are officially recognized and placed on a higher professional level.

Salary Adjustment

An upgrade results in higher salary levels and allowances.

Improved Quality of Education

Better-trained teachers help improve teaching quality in schools.

Step 1

Teacher Obtains Higher Qualification

The teacher completes a recognized program from an accredited institution such as:

Recognized Qualifications:
  • Diploma in Education
  • Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.)
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Education
  • Master's Degree in Education

Institutions must be recognized by the National Teaching Council and accepted by GES.

Step 2

Apply for Upgrading

The teacher applies through the GES upgrading portal or through the district education office. The application usually includes:

Application Information Required:
  • Staff ID
  • Current rank
  • New academic qualification
  • Date qualification was obtained
Submission of Required Documents:

Teachers must upload or submit certified copies of:

  • Degree or diploma certificate
  • Academic transcript
  • Appointment letter
  • Study leave approval (if applicable)
Apply on the Upgrading Portal

Step 3

Verification of Qualification

GES verifies the documents to ensure the certificate is genuine and recognized. Verification is done with:

1

The Awarding University

GES verifies the qualification directly with the institution that awarded the certificate.

2

The National Teaching Council

The NTC confirms the qualification is officially recognized and accepted for upgrading purposes.

Step 4

Approval and Rank Upgrade

Once verification is complete, GES:

  • Upgrades the teacher's professional rank
  • Adjusts the salary scale
  • Updates the teacher's records in the system
  • This depends on qualification and years of service

Portal Steps

Visit the GEDP-GES Upgrading Portal

Access the GES upgrading portal of Ghana Education Digital Platform and complete the following steps:

1

Fill the Application Form — provide all the required information accurately, including Personal Details, Professional Information, Qualification Details, and upload Supporting Documents.

2

Review Your Application — before submitting, check all information carefully, confirm the uploaded documents are correct, and make sure there are no spelling errors or missing fields.

3

Submit the Application — click Submit Application. The system generates a confirmation message and you may receive an application reference number. Save or screenshot this for tracking.

Incorrect information may delay approval.

Step 7

Application Review Process

Your application will be reviewed by the following authorities who will verify your qualification and documents:

1

District Education Office

First level of review at the district education level.

2

Regional Education Directorate

Regional-level assessment and recommendation.

3

GES Headquarters

Headquarters of the Ghana Education Service — final authority for all upgrading decisions.

Step 8

Approval and Upgrade

If approved:

  • Your rank will be upgraded
  • Your salary scale will be adjusted
  • Your records will be updated in the GES system
  • You may receive an official upgrading letter

Final

Summary

Teachers log into the GEDP portal, fill the upgrading form, upload certificates and documents, submit the application, and wait for verification and approval.
