Privacy & Cookies Policy

How we protect and manage your data on the GEDP platform.

Introduction.

The Ghana Education Digital Platform (GEDP) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect.

Personal Information: Name, email address, staff ID, and professional details provided during registration.

Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the platform, including log data and device information.

Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze platform traffic.

How We Use Your Information.

To provide and maintain our services, facilitate administrative and academic modules, communicate updates and support, and improve platform functionality and security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure.

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with relevant government agencies (e.g., Ghana Education Service) strictly for official administrative purposes.

Security.

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Cookies Policy.

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the platform to function.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand platform usage.

Preference Cookies: Remember your settings.

Your Rights.

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data.

Updates to this Policy.

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via the platform.
